DONAULA Handyman was built on a simple principle: do the best work, charge a fair price, and let the results bring the next customer. No gimmicks. No pushy sales. Just craftsmanship that earns trust.

Our Story

From One Man With a Hammer
to the Team You Can Trust

John started DONAULA Handyman with his own two hands, a truck, and a commitment to quality that most contractors have abandoned. Every early job was a chance to prove that craftsmanship still matters — and customers noticed.

Word spread. Referrals followed. Today, DONAULA serves homeowners across five counties — from the neighborhoods of Scarsdale and White Plains to the Bronx, Rockland County, and Connecticut. The crew has grown, the capabilities have expanded, but the standard hasn't changed.

Whether it's a specialty copper installation on a historic home or mounting a TV on a Saturday afternoon, the approach is the same: show up on time, do the work right, leave the space cleaner than we found it.
